Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new recipe?

Go to Recipes ‚Üí Add Recipe, fill in the details, and tap Save.

Can I create my own custom recipes?

Yes! Use the Create Recipe option to add your own ingredients, steps, and notes.

Where can I find recommended recipes?

Recommended recipes appear on the home screen under Recommended for You.

Can I edit or delete a recipe?

Yes! Open the recipe you want to modify, tap the Edit or Delete button, and confirm.

Can I use the app offline?

No, My Recipe Diary requires an internet connection to view, create, and manage recipes.

Can I upload my own cooking videos?

No, you cannot upload videos at the moment. However, you can watch pre-uploaded cooking videos available in the app.

Are my recipes backed up?

Yes! All your recipes are automatically saved in our cloud, linked to your email address as your unique ID.
